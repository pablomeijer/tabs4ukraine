Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 20, 2025

Tabs4Ukraine is a Chrome extension that displays non-intrusive advertisements to generate revenue for humanitarian support in Ukraine. We are committed to protecting your privacy and minimizing data collection.

What We Collect

  • Local preferences: Settings you change within the extension (e.g., showing shortcuts, theme, number of sponsored shortcuts) are stored using Chrome’s storage.sync/storage.local APIs.
  • Donation totals (local cache): We may cache the latest “total raised” number locally so it can be displayed quickly on new tabs.
  • Optional account data: If you sign up or sign in, we store your email and username in our backend (Supabase/Ethicly) to provide features like impact tracking and ad settings tied to your account.

We do not collect your browsing history. If you choose to show “most visited” shortcuts, we use Chrome’s built-in topSites API only on your device to render those entries. We do not transmit this list to our servers.

How Ads Work

Ads render in the new tab experience and/or sponsored shortcuts. Revenue from ad impressions and clicks supports humanitarian organizations aiding Ukraine. Ad creative may be served from our servers or trusted CDNs. We do not embed third-party trackers that fingerprint users.

Analytics

We aim to avoid invasive analytics. Limited, aggregate metrics (e.g., total impressions, total clicks) may be recorded server-side without storing personally identifiable browsing data. If you are logged in, impression/click counters may be associated to your user ID solely to compute your impact and prevent fraud.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We may share strictly necessary, aggregate metrics with partners for auditing donations and ad performance.
  • If legally required, we may disclose minimal information in response to valid legal requests.

Permissions

The extension requests Chrome permissions necessary to provide functionality (e.g., storage, topSites, bookmarks, activeTab). These are used to render UI features locally and do not grant us access to your private data without explicit user actions.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security controls, HTTPS, and access controls to protect data. Nevertheless, no system is 100% secure; use the extension at your discretion.

Data Retention

Local settings remain on your device until you uninstall/clear browser data. Account data persists while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Choices

  • Use the extension without creating an account.
  • Disable features like sponsored shortcuts in Settings.
  • Uninstall the extension at any time.
  • Request account deletion or data export by contacting us.

Children’s Privacy

Tabs4Ukraine is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us for removal.

Updates

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Material changes will be noted with a new effective date.
