Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 15, 2025

Tabs4Ukraine is a Chrome extension that displays non-intrusive advertisements to generate revenue for humanitarian support in Ukraine. We partner with verified humanitarian organizations including UNICEF, Save the Children, Doctors Without Borders, World Central Kitchen, the International Rescue Committee, and other trusted organizations supporting humanitarian relief efforts for Ukraine. We are committed to protecting your privacy and minimizing data collection.

What We Collect

  • Local preferences only: Settings you change within the extension (e.g., showing shortcuts, theme, number of sponsored shortcuts) are stored locally using Chrome's storage.sync/storage.local APIs.
  • Donation totals (local cache): We may cache the latest "total raised" number locally so it can be displayed quickly on new tabs.

We do not collect any personal information. No email addresses, usernames, or account data are collected or stored.

How Ads Work

Ads render in the new tab experience and/or sponsored shortcuts. Revenue from ad impressions and clicks supports humanitarian organizations aiding Ukraine. Ad creative may be served from our servers or trusted CDNs. We do not embed third-party trackers that fingerprint users.

Analytics

We aim to avoid invasive analytics. Limited, aggregate metrics (e.g., total impressions, total clicks) may be recorded server-side without storing personally identifiable browsing data. No user accounts exist, so no personal data is associated with analytics.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We may share strictly necessary, aggregate metrics with partners for auditing donations and ad performance.
  • If legally required, we may disclose minimal information in response to valid legal requests.

Permissions

The extension requests Chrome permissions necessary to provide functionality (e.g., storage, topSites, bookmarks, activeTab). These are used to render UI features locally and do not grant us access to your private data without explicit user actions.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security controls, HTTPS, and access controls to protect data. Nevertheless, no system is 100% secure; use the extension at your discretion.

Data Retention

Local settings remain on your device until you uninstall/clear browser data. No personal data is stored on our servers, so there is nothing to delete.

Your Choices

  • Disable features like sponsored shortcuts in Settings.
  • Uninstall the extension at any time.
  • Clear local data by uninstalling the extension.
  • Contact us with any questions or concerns.
